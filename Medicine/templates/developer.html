About the Project

This healthcare recommendation website is designed to assist users with medical needs. It offers:

  • Symptom-Based Recommendations: Enter symptoms to get suggestions for diseases, prevention, medicines, descriptions, diets, and more.
  • LLAMA Chatbot: Ask personalized medical queries and get expert guidance.
  • Find Doctors: Locate nearby doctors based on symptoms and location for immediate consultation.

This project was developed as part of the LNMHacks hackathon by a team of four talented individuals.
